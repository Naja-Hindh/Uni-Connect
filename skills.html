Master the Perfect Pitch

Every great project needs a great story. Follow these steps.

1

Problem Statement

Clearly define the pain point you are solving. Use data if possible.

2

The Solution

Explain your product and how it directly addresses the problem.

3

Target Audience

Who is this for? Be specific about your user persona.

4

Tech Stack

Showcase the technologies used. Mention any "secret sauce".

5

Business Model

How will it make money or sustain itself? (Freemium, Ads, SaaS)

6

Future Roadmap

What's next? Show ambition and long-term vision.
